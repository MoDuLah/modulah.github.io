Pythagoras Project

A focused company management workspace for Torn built to help directors handle training, staff history, analytics, reporting, backups, and operational planning in one place.

Under Development Join Discord

At a glance

  • Training ledger with configurable discounts and loyalty handling
  • FIFO train planner with sponsored rotation support
  • Staff and director history tracking with timeline views
  • Daily and weekly analytics summaries, exports, and themed reports

What it does

Pythagoras CIS gives you a structured workspace for company operations without relying on background scraping. Syncs are triggered manually, and data stays local unless you choose to export it.
