CORE GAME CONCEPT

Rise of the Hellenes is a persistent browser-based MMO where players:
* Create their character, by naming and selecting an avatar.
* Engage in missions (PVE)
* Engage in Arena (PvP)
* Engage in War (Clan PvP)
* Item Craft, repair, trade
* House building
* Specialized Origin (Athenian, Corinthean, Makedonian, )
* Gain fortune, fame, rank
* Rank points: Timē → rank (titles, privileges)
* Currencies (Premium: Ambrosia, Tetradrachmae (gold coin), Drachmae (silver coin), Ovolos (Bronze coin))
* Experience Points: Kleos → experience points (your legend)
* Reputation Points: Doxa → reputation points (cities, factions)
