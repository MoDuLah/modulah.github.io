Description

Race Tracker is a structured Google Sheets toolkit for Torn racers who want results, car history, upgrade notes, and performance review in one organised workbook.

What this toolkit does

Race history

Organises imported race results so positions, outcomes, and previous sessions are easier to review.

Car and track analysis

Connects cars, tracks, classes, and upgrades to the results you are recording.

Garage planning

Gives your garage decisions context by keeping performance data close to upgrade tracking.

Performance summaries

Turns racing records into dashboard-style views for spotting trends and choosing the next move.
