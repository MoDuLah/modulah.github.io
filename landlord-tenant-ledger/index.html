Landlord Tenant Ledger

A simple Torn property helper for seeing who rents what, what is ending soon, and what your own homes may be worth listing for.

Under development Back to Supporter Scripts Join Discord

At a glance

  • See rented homes, renters, prices, dates, and days left in one place
  • Keep partner homes and shared homes from showing twice
  • Get rent or sale suggestions for homes you personally own

Status

Landlord Tenant Ledger is under development and is not public yet.

The public download has been removed until the green light is given. Private testing will be shared through Discord when it is ready.

Access

Ledger access will be its own LTL pass. It will not use the Pit Guru pass.

Setup will show whether the Torn user from the saved Torn key has Ledger access, so players can tell straight away if their access is active.

What this tool does

Leases

Shows home type, owner, renter, rent total, rent per day, start and end dates, days left, status, and notes.

Fetching

Can fill homes you own, the home you live in, and partner homes when Torn has that information available.

Suggestions

Can suggest whether your own homes should be rented out or sold, with a suggested rental length where useful.

Safer editing

Owner and days-left values come from Torn or from the dates. The user should not have to edit those by hand.

Why it is still private

The tool is being made clearer before release. The ledger should stay in place while editing, avoid duplicate homes, use simple status names, and make manual entries easier to fill.

Price checks and suggestions should happen quietly in the background, so the user only sees the answer instead of a confusing wall of scan results.
