Pythagoras Project

A focused company management workspace for Torn, built to help directors handle training, staff history, analytics, reporting, backups, and operational planning in one place.

📘Open FAQ 💬Join Discord

At a glance

  • Training ledger with configurable discounts
  • FIFO train planner and sponsored rotation support
  • Staff and director history tracking
  • Daily and weekly analytics summaries
  • Backups, exports, reports, and themed output

What it does

Pythagoras CIS gives you a structured workspace for company operations without relying on background scraping. Syncs are triggered manually, and data remains local unless you choose to export it.
