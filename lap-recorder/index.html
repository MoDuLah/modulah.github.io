Description

Lap Recorder is a Torn racing helper focused on capturing lap times, keeping track and car records visible, and giving each session enough context to review later.

What this script does

Lap capture

Records racing laps with the context needed to compare sessions cleanly.

Track and car records

Keeps lap records tied to the car and track they belong to instead of mixing everything together.

Replay context

Helps connect recorded times with replay-aware details for easier post-race review.

Session history

Gives racers a clearer trail of recent performance without turning the race page into a spreadsheet.
