Smuggler

Description

Smuggler is a live Torn travel workflow helper built around repeated abroad runs. It helps with destination setup, item priorities, purchase flow, travel timing, and the next natural page action without hiding what Torn is asking you to confirm.

What this script does

Travel pill

Adds a compact draggable pill with route context, countdown, ETA, and progress while you move around Torn.

Item workflow

Supports country selection, category ordering, and primary and fallback item choices for abroad shopping.

Step-based progression

Advances through the next natural travel or buy step one action at a time instead of blindly chaining the whole run.

Travel safeguards

Checks common blockers before travel and keeps return-home behaviour aware of whether the trip has actually produced a purchase.
