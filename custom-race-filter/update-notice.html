Licensing Has Moved

Hello {TORN_NAME},

Licensing for MoDuL scripts has now moved to Discord.

To verify your access, please join:

Join MoDuL Discord

You will need a public Torn API key.

Once you are in the server, type:

/verify

A popup will appear where you can paste your public API key. After verification, you will receive the Verified role and your Discord nickname will be updated to your Torn name and ID.

Please make sure DMs from the server are enabled.

After that, type:

/buylicense

You will be shown a private product selection menu. Choose the script you want, follow the bot instructions, and send the required XAN or money using the generated trigger word.

Example trigger word:

!CRF-000055

The bot will check the payment logs and automatically give you access.

No manual license keys are needed anymore.

This message will only be shown once.
